    • What is EcoChange?


    EcoChange is an innovative application that encourages users to connect with others across the globe in the interest of
    preventing climate change for future generations. With an easy to use interface, users are motivated to accomplish simple
    tasks in their living environment in exchange for prizes and most importantly, an Earth free from climate change.



    Daily and Weekly Quests


    EcoChange tracks users’ involvement in environmental tasks through “Quests”; sets of challenges to
    get the user to contribute within the community. Daily quests consist of mini tasks which refresh
    everyday, while Weekly quests are goals to complete throughout longer periods of time. The aim of the
    quest system is to motivate the user to participate consistently in promoting and creating sustainability
    for the environment. Examples of Weekly quests may include observing the growth of a plant throughout
    each week, including Daily quests such as planting, watering, and taking care of the plant. Completing
    quests will grant users ecocoins, a virtual currency that can be traded in for rewards such as gift cards.
    Weekly quests give more ecocoins than Daily quests, encouraging users to complete long-term tasks that may
    be more impactful and beneficial to the environment.

    Exchange EcoCoins for Awesome Prizes


    Users are rewarded with EcoCoins every time they complete tasks, as well as daily quests and weekly
    challenges. Once users have collected enough EcoCoins, they are able to exchange the virtual currency
    at the EcoChange Shop to choose from a wide variety of prizes and rewards. Users can exchange the EcoCoins
    to obtain special gift cards from various companies, collectibles and merchandise, as well as customizable
    features to their own profile! EcoChange also comes with a badge and achievement system. Complete
    different tasks through the daily and weekly quests and obtain shiny badges and collectibles to display
    in your personal profile. Earn prestigious achievements by keeping streaks or completing specific tasks!
    There may even be some secret badges and achievements waiting to be unlocked…



    Compete with Friends and other Users


    Friend System/Leaderboard: Creating environmental change alone can seem like a daunting task.
    EcoChange’s friend leaderboard function allows users across the world to develop a network in order to
    support and challenge one another to constantly improve. The updated weekly friend leaderboard system
    allows users to see their friends’ contributions to solving the climate crisis every day, motivating users
    to engage in friendly competition with their peers.

    World Leaderboard: The world leaderboard allows users to compete with other planet savers on a global
    scale. Users will be placed in different leagues according to their Ecocoin amount, and they can work their way
    up the ranks as they complete more tasks! There are 7 leagues in total (listed from the lowest to the highest):
    Seed 3, Seed 2, Seed 1, Sprout 3, Sprout 2, Sprout 1, and Flower. The leaderboard will refresh weekly and users
    will receive Ecocoins as prizes according to their ranks in the leagues.



    Professional Verification System

    Upon completing each task or quest, users are required to provide proof of contribution through EcoChange’s
    implementation of human moderators. Users are able to send photos and videos to our moderators, who work
    24/7 to ensure that users are able to obtain their rewards in a timely manner. This system also prevents
    possible abuse of the reward system.
