    • About Us


    Our EcoChange team is a group of high school students who are passionate about helping the environment and
    combatting climate change. We strive to motivate others to start caring for the environment with the
    EcoChange app, as well as popularizing sustainable lifestyles in communities. We were inspired to design
    this app because of Joe, a high school student from the Philippines where climate change is extremely
    severe. Read below more about his story and how the EcoChange team is working to support his community!



    Joe's Story

    Joe is a high school student from the Philippines where his hometown is at risk of facing extreme
    weather conditions. Joe is aware that the rapidly increasing population in his area has had its
    tolls,and is threatening the country’s vulnerability to climate change. Some of the trends that
    have amplified the environmental crisis include waste and housing problems, deforestation, and
    rising CO2 emissions.



    Helping Joe and his community

    In 10-20 years, the consequences of climate change will become irreversible. Given that the
    community has an increasing population, the contribution to global warming is higher than ever,
    which also means more intense consequences. To minimize the catastrophes of climate change, it is
    important to take advantage of the available large population. With the help of EcoChange, Joe’s
    community is able to start improving their current situation by identifying the factors
    amplifying climate change (waste production, deforestation, rising carbon emissions, etc) and
    solving them one task at a time.
